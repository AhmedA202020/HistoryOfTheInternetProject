Communication Via The Internet

This video talks about how people use the internet as a way of communicating with one another. It all

started with a company called napster founded by shawn fanning. Napster is a website that was

created in 1999 where people can share mp3 versions of songs with other people so they can

download them and listen to them at anytime without paying a penny, people could also

communicate with others on napster regrading songs. This caused anger from artists and record

store owners because no one would buy records anymore since they can just go to napster and

download the songs for free. They ended up going to court and napster ended up shutting down in

2000 for copyright issues. Then came myspace, a website where people can share things about

themselves and communicate with others. It was mostly used for dating though. Myspace was

created in 2003 but then came facebook in 2004 and absolutely destroyed myspace in a short

period of time because facebook was just easier to use and had way more features than myspace.
